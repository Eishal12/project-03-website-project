# Final project scope statement- photography portfolio

## Purpose
- create a clean, minimal photography portfolio that showcase my work.
- Highlight four categories: nature, city scene, nighshots, and everyday moments.
- Provide a simple online space where viewers can explore my photos easily.

## Main features
- A welcoming homepage with a short introduction.
- A gallery section divided into the four photography categories.
- simple navigation across all pages. 
- an email link for anyone who wants to reach out or give feedback.

## Audience
- anyone interested in photography, including general viewers, friends, and family.

## out of scope
- no e-commerce feature.
- no blogs or longform writing section.
- no user accounts or login system.
- no heavy animations or complex interactive element.

## project summary
this scope statement defines the goals, boundaries, and structure of the final project. It outlines the purpose of the photography portfolio, the features included, and the limitations of the project.
